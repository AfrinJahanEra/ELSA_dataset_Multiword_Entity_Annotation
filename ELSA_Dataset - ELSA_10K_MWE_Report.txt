======================================================================
  SECTION 9 — METHODOLOGY & THEORETICAL NOTES
======================================================================

  Detection Method:
    Multi-word entities were detected by identifying consecutive
    _NE_-prefixed tokens in the ENTITY_TAGGED_REVIEW column.
    Two or more adjacent _NE_ tokens form one MWE span.

  SUB Classification:
    A single-token NE is classified as SUB if its clean surface
    form appears as a constituent token in ANY MWE found in the
    entire dataset (document-level inventory lookup).

  BIOES Tagging:
    Applied per the BIOES scheme standard in NER literature.
    Single token → S; First of multi → B; Last of multi → E;
    Any middle token(s) → I.

  Theoretical basis:
    Hu et al. (AAAI 2020) showed that occurrences of a token
    within a multi-token entity provide more reliable contextual
    signal for disambiguating single-token occurrences of the
    same surface form elsewhere in the document.

  Dataset language: Bengali (e-commerce product reviews)
  NE type: Product entities (clothing, books, cosmetics, etc.)
